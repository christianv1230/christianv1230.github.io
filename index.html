Ingredients:

  • Lamb / Chicken - 1 Kilo
  • Large Onions - 4
  • Whole Spices
  • Yougurt - 4 Tablespoons

Recipe:

Meat : Lamb / Chicken with or without bone
Yogurt for marination
Whole spices - we will fish them out before final stage
Onions
Marinate meat in yogurt
Soak rice in water
Chop onions in round circles
Mint
Coriander
Powdered spices
Garam Masala
Ghee / Clarified Butter
Heat oil and ghee in a pot and add whole spices
Add all of the onions to the pot
We want to caramelize the onions and not burn them
Onions are now caramelized and we take them out of the pot
Add marinated meat to the pot
Fish out all the big whole spices and put them away - we will not need them
Cook the marinated meat for 5 to 10 minutes
Half of the onions go in the pot and half of them stay for the last
Ginger and garlic go into the pot
Powdered spices go into the pot + salt for the curry
Half of the mint leaves + half of the coriander go in the pot
Cook meat until 80% done. Lamb leg / shoulder needs 15 minutes pressure cooking
Rose water (optional)
Finely chop mint and coriander
Open the pot, once pressure cooking is complete
Now to start layering up in a new pot
